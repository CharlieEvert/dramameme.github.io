How it works

A simple creator-controlled workflow.

Dramameme is designed for creators who want speed without giving up review and control.

01 · Create

Generate an episode

The creator starts with a meme drama idea, topic, or template. Dramameme helps generate a short video concept, caption, and clip.

02 · Review

Approve the final version

The creator reviews the video, title, caption, hashtags, and privacy settings. Nothing is sent to TikTok without user action.

03 · Post

Publish through TikTok

After authorization and approval, Dramameme uses TikTok’s posting APIs to publish the creator-approved video to the creator’s TikTok account.

Trust & safety

No hidden or automatic posting.

Dramameme is built around creator review. Users are responsible for their content and must follow TikTok’s terms, community guidelines, and developer policies.

Creator control

Users must connect their own TikTok account, review the generated media, and intentionally approve publishing. Dramameme does not post without authorization and approval.

AI-generated content

Dramameme creates AI-assisted content. Generated videos should be reviewed before publishing and may be labeled or handled according to platform requirements.

Data minimization

Dramameme uses TikTok account data and posting permissions only for account connection, review, and creator-approved publishing.
