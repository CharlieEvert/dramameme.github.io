Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. Overview

Dramameme respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information Dramameme may collect, how it is used, and how you can contact us about privacy-related requests.

2. Information we may collect

When you use Dramameme, we may process information such as account connection status, TikTok basic account information made available through TikTok authorization, authorization tokens, generated video files, captions, hashtags, selected privacy settings, publishing metadata, support requests, and basic website usage information.

3. TikTok account connection

If you connect your TikTok account, Dramameme may receive basic TikTok account information and authorization tokens from TikTok. We use this information only to connect your account, display connected account status, and publish videos that you review and approve.

4. How we use information

We use information to operate Dramameme, generate and preview content, support account connection, publish creator-approved videos, provide support, protect the service from abuse, and comply with legal or platform requirements.

5. No sale of personal information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not post to your TikTok account unless you authorize TikTok access and approve the publishing action.

6. Service providers

Dramameme may use service providers for hosting, storage, media processing, analytics, authentication, and publishing integrations. These providers may process information only as needed to provide the service.

7. Data retention and deletion

We keep app-related information only as long as needed to operate the service, provide support, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of connected account information or app-related data by contacting us.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. However, no internet service, hosting provider, or data transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Children

Dramameme is not intended for children under 13. Users must meet the age requirements of any third-party platform they connect, including TikTok.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

For privacy requests, account disconnect help, or deletion requests, contact charlieevert@gmail.com.
